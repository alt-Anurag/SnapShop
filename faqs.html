Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SnapShop's visual shopping assistant.

SnapShop uses advanced computer vision and machine learning to analyze the images you upload. It identifies objects, patterns, and styles in your photos, then matches them with similar products from our database. You'll receive personalized recommendations based on your visual preferences.

For best results, use clear, well-lit photos with a single main subject. Images with minimal background clutter and good contrast tend to produce the most accurate matches. You can upload photos of clothing, accessories, home decor, or any other products you're interested in.

We respect your privacy. Images are processed temporarily to generate recommendations and are not stored long-term. We don't use your images for any other purpose beyond providing the shopping recommendations you request. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

When you find a product you like, click the "Click to Buy" button. This will take you directly to the retailer's website where you can complete your purchase. SnapShop doesn't handle transactions directly but connects you with trusted retailers.

While you can use SnapShop without an account, creating one allows us to save your preferences and search history. This helps us provide better personalized recommendations over time. An account also lets you save favorite products and access them across devices.

We partner with hundreds of retailers across fashion, home goods, electronics, and more. Our database includes products from major brands as well as unique items from smaller boutiques. We're constantly adding new partners to expand your shopping options.
